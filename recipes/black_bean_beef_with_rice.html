Description of recipe:

This chicken burger is meticulously designed to tingle your taste buds. Grilled chicken breasts are grilled on a wooden stove, after being marinated for 12 hours with a mixture of secret ingredients

After being grilled, it is sandwiched between two grilled patties that are extremely soft. Following this, lettuce, tomatoes, and cucumber are placed on top of melting cheese to create a succulent and tasty burger.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken Breast Fillet
  • Lettuce
  • Cucumber
  • Tomato
  • Cheese

Steps:

  1. Take chicken and place on grill
  2. Cook chicken for 8 minutes, flipping half way
  3. Grill the buns after flipping once until chicken is finished (4 minutes)
  4. After chicken and buns are finished, place cheese and cucumber on top of chicken and then place into buns
